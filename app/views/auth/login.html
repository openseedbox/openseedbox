Common login questions


Why dont you support login with Facebook?

Facebook doesnt support OpenID. We only support OpenID because of its simplicity and the fact that we just want login and not any of the services of the providing website. If Facebook starts supporting OpenID, then we will start supporting Facebook login.

Why dont you allow account creation?

We hate remembering a separate set of credentials for every website we visit. Thats why we use OpenID. Chances are you already have a Google account, or an account with a supported OpenID provider. If you dont, we suggest you create one.

I'm sick of typing my OpenID url, can you please add my provider to the list of supported providers?

Sure, send an email to support@openseedbox.com and we'll see what we can do
